About

Building software with mathematical discipline.

I am a full-stack developer and mathematics enthusiast from Greece. My work combines practical engineering with analytical problem solving: designing application architecture, building frontend and backend systems, working with APIs, and exploring security, computer vision, and machine learning methods.

I am now shaping this experience into a research direction. I am especially interested in PhD work where rigorous methods can improve real software systems: safer platforms, better intelligent services, computer vision applications, and tools that remain useful outside the laboratory.
